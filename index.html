About Saraswati Public School

Blessed by the vision and guidance of Shri Late Netra Singh Rautela, Saraswati Public School stands as a symbol of quality education and strong values in our community.

Established in 2003, the school has continuously worked towards nurturing every child's unique potential through a balanced focus on academics, arts, sports, and moral values.

Our mission is to create a stimulating and supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their interests, develop critical thinking, and grow into confident, responsible global citizens.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a caring environment, and a team of dedicated educators, Saraswati Public School provides a comprehensive and enriching educational experience from kindergarten to high school.

Annual Events

Celebrating achievements and fostering creativity through school events

December

Annual Function

The Annual Function is the highlight of our school year, showcasing the diverse talents of our students through music, dance, drama, and cultural performances. This grand celebration brings together students, parents, teachers, and alumni in a festive atmosphere that highlights our school's achievements and cultural diversity.

Featuring award ceremonies, special guest performances, and presentations by all grade levels, this event celebrates the holistic development of our students throughout the academic year.

January

Annual Sports Day

Annual Sports Day promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students. This energetic event includes track and field competitions, team sports, and fun activities for all age groups.

Students participate in various athletic events including races, relay competitions, long jump, high jump, and team games. The day culminates with award ceremonies recognizing outstanding athletic achievements and team spirit.

February

Science & Arts Exhibition Day

This dual exhibition showcases the innovative scientific projects and creative artistic talents of our students. The Science Exhibition features working models, experiments, and research projects across various scientific disciplines.

The Arts Exhibition displays paintings, sculptures, crafts, and digital art created by students throughout the year. This event encourages creative thinking, scientific inquiry, and appreciation for both arts and sciences among students and visitors.
