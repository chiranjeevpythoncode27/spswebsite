About Our Heritage

Blessed by the vision of Late Shri Netra Singh Rautela & Late Smt Sarswati Rautela, Sarswati Public School stands as a beacon of quality education, rooted in moral values and academic brilliance. Established in 2003, we nurture young minds with a perfect blend of tradition and modernity, empowering students for global opportunities while preserving cultural ethos.

Our mission is to create compassionate, inquisitive, and resilient leaders through a dynamic curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and dedicated mentorship.
